3D sampling & Rendering

What we offer

To give you a quick start and lower your risks, ShareCloth Services Team can provide you with services of creating 3D sampling of your clothing products on the basis of digital patterns and/or photos of your collection.

Our professional team of 3D, graphic and garment designers will quickly create precise virtual samples for you.

As a result of this collaboration, you will receive the created 3D mockups to your ShareCloth Cloud account, thus enabling you to use these projects within the framework of both products: you can continue working on the item’s design or use the generated 3D mockups in your online retail business.
